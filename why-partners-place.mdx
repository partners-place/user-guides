# Why Partners Place?

## The Challenge of U.S. Market Entry

Entering the U.S. market presents unique challenges for Korean startups:

- **Complex Legal Requirements**: Navigating U.S. corporate law, tax regulations, and compliance
- **Banking Barriers**: Opening U.S. business accounts from abroad
- **Sales Infrastructure**: Setting up payment processing and local operations
- **Time and Cost**: Traditional approaches require significant investment before validation
