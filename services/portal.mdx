## Why Delaware?

### 🏛️ Business-Friendly Laws
Delaware offers the most developed and predictable corporate law system in the U.S.

### ⚖️ Court of Chancery
Specialized business court with judges expert in corporate matters

### 🔒 Privacy Protection
No requirement to disclose officer or director names publicly

### 💰 Tax Efficiency
No state sales tax and favorable corporate tax structure
